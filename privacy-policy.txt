# Privacy Policy

We value your privacy. This extension does not collect or store any personal data. All selected text is processed securely and only when you explicitly request summarization or code explanation.

## Data We Process
- Selected text: Sent to our AI API for summarization or explanation.
- Email: Used only for verifying Pro subscriptions.

## Data We Do NOT Collect
- Browsing history
- Personal information beyond email (for Pro users)

## Third-Party Services
- Stripe: Handles payments securely.
- AI API: Processes selected text for summarization or explanation.

For questions, contact support@example.com.
